# Human Design reference pack

Reference material for writing packet prose. **This is source material, not final
prose**: every packet's readings are written fresh per person and grounded in that
person's computed chart, then run through the `/de-ai` checklist. Do not paste these
keynotes verbatim into a packet.

**Provenance & caution.** The Type / Strategy / Authority / Profile / Center / Definition
material below is stable canon across the authoritative sources (Ra Uru Hu & Lynda
Bunnell, *The Definitive Book of Human Design*; Jovian Archive). The **64 gate keynotes**
have the most wording drift between schools, and the **incarnation-cross names** live in
`crosses.json` (sourced separately). Before anything ships in a delivered packet,
spot-check the specific person's active gate keynotes and their exact cross name against
Jovian Archive or the Definitive Book. Do not mix in Richard Rudd's Gene Keys naming
(a different Shadow/Gift/Siddhi system).

## 1. The five Types

**Manifestor** (~8–9%). Aura: closed, repelling. Strategy: **to inform** before acting.
Signature: **Peace**. Not-Self: **Anger**. The only type built to initiate without
waiting; a motor wired to the Throat gives direct manifesting power. Not built to be
consistent workers or to be managed. Informing dissolves the resistance their impactful
aura would otherwise provoke.

**Generator** (~37%; with MGs, Sacral types are ~70% of people). Aura: open, enveloping.
Strategy: **to respond** (wait for something to respond to, let the Sacral answer).
Signature: **Satisfaction**. Not-Self: **Frustration**. Defined Sacral = the life-force
/ work engine. Built to master work they love through sustainable, responsive energy.
Initiating instead of responding leads to frustration and quitting.

**Manifesting Generator** (~32–33%). Aura: open, enveloping. Strategy: **respond, then
inform**. Signature: **Satisfaction** (some add Peace). Not-Self: **Frustration** (and
Anger). A Generator (defined Sacral) that also has a motor connected to the Throat. Fast,
multi-passionate, skips steps and circles back. Responds like a Generator, moves with
Manifestor speed.

**Projector** (~20–22%). Aura: focused, absorbing (penetrates and reads the other).
Strategy: **wait for the invitation** (for the big things: love, work, place,
recognition). Signature: **Success**. Not-Self: **Bitterness**. No defined Sacral, no
motor to the Throat: not a consistent energy type. Designed to guide, direct, and manage
others' energy, to see the other efficiently. The gift only lands when recognized and
invited; pushing uninvited yields exhaustion and bitterness.

**Reflector** (~1%). Aura: resistant, sampling (teflon-like, mirrors the environment).
Strategy: **wait a lunar cycle** (~28–29 days) before major decisions. Signature:
**Surprise / Delight**. Not-Self: **Disappointment**. All nine centers undefined; no
consistent inner definition. Samples and mirrors the health of their community, and is
transited by the Moon. Place and community matter more than for any other type.

## 2. The seven Authorities (highest defined center wins)

- **Emotional / Solar Plexus** (most common): the wave. No truth in the now; ride the
  emotional wave over time before deciding. Sleep on it; never decide at a peak or trough.
- **Sacral**: the gut responds in the moment, a felt "uh-huh" (yes) or "unh-unh" (no).
  Trust the immediate sound over mental reasoning.
- **Splenic**: the Spleen speaks once, quietly, in the present, a spontaneous flash of
  intuition. It does not repeat. Trust that first quiet knowing.
- **Ego / Heart / Will** (rare): decide from genuine want and willpower to commit. Truth
  surfaces through talking it out ("do I want this? will I do it?").
- **Self-Projected / G**: truth comes through hearing their own voice; talk it out with a
  trusted sounding board and listen to what they say and how it sounds.
- **Mental / Environmental / No Inner Authority**: no reliable inner authority; clarity
  comes from talking things through over time with trusted people in the right
  environment. Others don't decide for them; discussion externalizes the mind.
- **Lunar / Reflector**: wait a full ~28-day cycle, sampling the decision from every angle
  before committing.

## 3. The twelve Profiles

Line themes: **1 Investigator** (foundation, study), **2 Hermit** (natural gift called
out; needs solitude), **3 Martyr** (trial-and-error, resilient), **4 Opportunist /
Networker** (influence through relationships), **5 Heretic** (projected upon; universal
solutions), **6 Role Model** (three phases: trial → on the roof ~30–50 → embodied wisdom).

Read as **Personality-Sun line / Design-Sun line** = **conscious / unconscious**. First
number: what you know about yourself (mind). Second: the body, what others see first.

- **1/3** Investigator-Martyr: builds a foundation, then tests it by trial and error.
- **1/4** Investigator-Opportunist: foundational knowledge shared through a fixed network.
- **2/4** Hermit-Opportunist: natural talent called out through the network; easy to like.
- **2/5** Hermit-Heretic: a called-out gift meets projection and the demand for solutions.
- **3/5** Martyr-Heretic: trial-and-error turned into universal solutions; highly
  projected upon.
- **3/6** Martyr-Role Model: experiential early, then withdrawal, then embodied wisdom.
- **4/6** Opportunist-Role Model: network and loyalty matter; trusts, observes, models.
- **4/1** Opportunist-Investigator: the lone **Juxtaposition / fixed-fate** profile;
  fixed knowledge through fixed relationships, mission-like.
- **5/1** Heretic-Investigator: projected-upon problem-solver grounded in deep study.
- **5/2** Heretic-Hermit: projected-upon universalizer with a naturally called-out gift.
- **6/2** Role Model-Hermit: withdrawn wisdom-in-progress others recognize early.
- **6/3** Role Model-Martyr: the most experiential role model; wisdom hard-won.

## 4. The nine Centers (defined vs open, and the open center's not-self question)

- **Head** (pressure: inspiration, mental pressure, questions). Defined: fixed way of
  being inspired. Open: amplifies others' questions. Not-self: *am I chasing questions
  that aren't mine?*
- **Ajna** (awareness: conceptualization, certainty). Defined: fixed way of thinking (can
  be dogmatic). Open: flexible, open-minded; pressure to appear certain. Not-self: *am I
  pretending to be certain?*
- **Throat** (manifestation: speaking, doing). Defined: consistent voice/action. Open:
  inconsistent expression; may talk for attention. Not-self: *am I talking to be seen?*
- **G / Identity** (love, direction, self). Defined: stable identity and direction. Open:
  identity shifts with people and place; environment-sensitive. Not-self: *am I searching
  for love or a direction I think I should have?*
- **Ego / Heart / Will** (willpower, self-worth, promises). Defined: reliable willpower.
  Open: inconsistent; shouldn't make willpower promises. Not-self: *do I have something to
  prove?*
- **Sacral** (life force, work, sexuality). Defined: sustainable work energy. Open: no
  consistent life force; can overwork and burn out. Not-self: *do I know when enough is
  enough?*
- **Solar Plexus** (emotions, moods, the wave). Defined: rides its own wave; emotional
  authority. Open: absorbs others' emotions; avoids confrontation. Not-self: *am I
  avoiding truth to keep the peace?*
- **Spleen** (intuition, instinct, survival, health, fear, the present). Defined: steady
  intuition and well-being. Open: holds on out of fear; amplifies fear. Not-self: *am I
  holding on to something that isn't good for me?*
- **Root** (pressure: adrenaline, stress, drive). Defined: fixed way with pressure. Open:
  amplifies pressure; rushes to be free of it. Not-self: *am I hurrying just to be free
  of the pressure?*

## 5. Definition

- **Single**: all defined centers form one circuit; self-contained, consistent.
- **Split**: two separate areas, no channel joining them; drawn to what bridges the gap
  (specific gates/people feel completing).
- **Triple-Split**: three areas; busy, multi-threaded; bridged by many people/environments.
- **Quadruple-Split**: four areas (the maximum); needs many influences; can take longer to
  feel integrated.
- **No Definition** (Reflector): nothing fixed; wholeness comes from environment and the
  lunar cycle.

## 7. Incarnation Cross

Built from the four Sun/Earth arms: **Personality Sun / Personality Earth | Design Sun /
Design Earth** (Sun and Earth are always opposite gates). Roughly 70% of the imprint is
the Sun/Earth, which is why the cross carries so much weight. Naming:
*[Angle] Cross of [Name] (pSun/pEarth | dSun/dEarth)*.

Angle from the profile:
- **Right Angle** (personal destiny; learns through their own life): 1/3, 1/4, 2/4, 2/5,
  3/5, 3/6, 4/6.
- **Juxtaposition** (fixed fate): 4/1 only.
- **Left Angle** (transpersonal karma; worked out through others): 5/1, 5/2, 6/2, 6/3.

Cross names live in `pipeline/crosses.json`, keyed `"pSun/pEarth|dSun/dEarth"`. If a
cross isn't in the table, show the gates and angle and omit the name rather than guess.

## 8. The Variables / Primary Health System (advanced)

The finest layer, from **Color** and **Tone** (the two subdivisions below the line). The
five keynotes come from 4 colors + 1 tone off two point-pairs; the four crown arrows come
from tone (1-3 = Left, 4-6 = Right). Computed by `pipeline/variables.py`; keynotes below are
Jovian-order. **This is the most birth-time-sensitive layer** (a Tone shifts ~40 min, a
Color ~4 hr), taught last, after Type/Strategy/Authority are lived. Present with that caveat.

- **Determination (Digestion)** = COLOR of Design Sun. 1 Appetite · 2 Taste · 3 Thirst ·
  4 Touch · 5 Sound · 6 Light. Tone splits Active (1-3) / Passive (4-6). *How you take in
  food and information.*
- **Cognition (Design Sense)** = TONE of Design Sun. 1 Smell · 2 Taste · 3 Outer Vision ·
  4 Inner Vision · 5 Feeling · 6 Touch. *The sense you trust.*
- **Environment** = COLOR of Design Nodes. 1 Caves · 2 Markets · 3 Kitchens · 4 Mountains ·
  5 Valleys · 6 Shores. Tone: Observed / Observer. *Where you thrive.*
- **Motivation** = COLOR of Personality Sun. 1 Fear · 2 Hope · 3 Desire · 4 Need · 5 Guilt ·
  6 Innocence. Tone: Strategic / Receptive. *The deepest "why" under the mind; meant to stay
  background, not managed.* (Fear = drive to face the unknown and get clear, not anxiety.)
- **Perspective (View)** = COLOR of Personality Nodes. 1 Survival · 2 Possibility · 3 Power ·
  4 Wanting · 5 Probability · 6 Personal. Tone: Focused / Peripheral. *How the mind frames
  what it sees.* (Some schools label 6 "Distraction/Wonder"; use Jovian's "Personal.")

Four-arrow code `P__ D__`: Personality (Motivation, Perspective) then Design (Determination,
Environment); Left = active/focused/directed, Right = receptive/peripheral/taking-in.

## 9. Honest framing (for the disclaimer)

Human Design is a self-reflective, experiential system, not a proven science. Ra Uru Hu's
own framing: *"Don't believe it. Just experiment with it and see for yourself."* It
synthesizes the I Ching (64 gates), astrology (birth and ~88 days prior), the chakra
system (9 centers), the Kabbalistic Tree of Life (channels), and a quantum/neutrino
framing. It has not been scientifically validated and is not a diagnosis or a prediction.

Adaptable disclaimer: *"Human Design is a self-reflective system that synthesizes the I
Ching, astrology, the chakras, and the Kabbalah. It is not scientifically validated and
is not a diagnosis or a prediction. In the words of its founder, the invitation is not to
believe any of it but to experiment and keep only what proves true in your own
experience."*
